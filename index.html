Hello! My name is Abby Biggs, and this is my personal website. I am from Michigan, but spent all of high school and middle school in Shanghai. I am a third year student at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Behavioral Neuroscience. I am passionate about aging and neurodegeneration research, specifically about how I can apply data tools to understand neurodegeneration. I currently work part time at the Sperling Lab, where I am creating a data pipeline to integrate newly collected study data into a shared data base. I am also working on a project analyzing Heart Rate Variability characteristics and how they might attenuate Alzheimer's Disease. In July, I'll be starting a new co-op at the Sinclair lab, working under a phd student on a project that aims to understand how peripheral neurons influence the immune system. I will be working there until December. In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with friends and family, travelling, running, and cooking. My favorite place I've travelled to is Australia, and a place I would love to travel to is Brazil. Another goal of mine this year is to run a half marathon in May. Thank you for reading all about me!
